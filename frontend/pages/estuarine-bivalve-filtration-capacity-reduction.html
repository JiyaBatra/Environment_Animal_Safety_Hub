Estuarine Bivalve Filtration Capacity Reduction

Analyzing Water Clarity Decline

Overview

Estuarine bivalves, such as oysters and clams, play a crucial role in maintaining water clarity through their filtration activities. However, various environmental stressors are reducing their filtration capacity, leading to declining water clarity in estuarine ecosystems.
