Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit a report?
Your report is reviewed within 24-48 hours. If urgent, it's forwarded to appropriate authorities immediately. You'll receive a tracking number to follow up.
Can I report anonymously?
Yes! You can check the "Report anonymously" box. However, providing contact information helps us follow up and gather more details if needed.
What should I do in an emergency?
Call emergency services (911) immediately if there's immediate danger to people, animals, or the environment. Then use our emergency hotline.
How do I know if my report was acted upon?
You'll receive updates via email. You can also check your report status using your tracking number on our website.
